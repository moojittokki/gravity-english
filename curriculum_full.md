# Gravity English: Full Curriculum Blueprint

This document outlines the expanded curriculum structure for Gravity English, designed for massive scale content generation. Each topic is a module for vocabulary, sentence practice, and interactive exercises.

---

## **Level A1: True Beginner**

**Goal:** Master survival English. Understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases. Introduce self and others, ask and answer basic questions about personal details.

**Total Target Words:** ~2,400

### A1 Topics
1.  **A1-01: Greetings & Introductions** (Target: 50 words)
2.  **A1-02: The Alphabet & Spelling** (Target: 50 words)
3.  **A1-03: Numbers 1-100** (Target: 75 words)
4.  **A1-04: Colors** (Target: 50 words)
5.  **A1-05: Basic Nouns (Objects)** (Target: 100 words)
6.  **A1-06: Basic Verbs (Action)** (Target: 100 words)
7.  **A1-07: Personal Information** (Name, Country) (Target: 60 words)
8.  **A1-08: Family Members** (Target: 60 words)
9.  **A1-09: Days, Months, & Dates** (Target: 75 words)
10. **A1-10: Telling Time** (Simple) (Target: 70 words)
11. **A1-11: Food & Drink** (Basics) (Target: 100 words)
12. **A1-12: Basic Clothes** (Target: 80 words)
13. **A1-13: Simple Body Parts** (Target: 60 words)
14. **A1-14: Weather** (Basic descriptions) (Target: 50 words)
15. **A1-15: Common Animals** (Pets, Farm) (Target: 80 words)
16. **A1-16: At the Cafe** (Simple ordering) (Target: 90 words)
17. **A1-17: Simple Prepositions** (in, on, under) (Target: 70 words)
18. **A1-18: Subject Pronouns & 'to be'** (I am, you are) (Target: 100 words)
19. **A1-19: Plural Nouns** (Regular) (Target: 100 words)
20. **A1-20: There is / There are** (Target: 90 words)
21. **A1-21: Basic Adjectives** (big, small, happy) (Target: 100 words)
22. **A1-22: Transportation** (car, bus, train) (Target: 80 words)
23. **A1-23: Simple Hobbies** (read, swim, watch TV) (Target: 70 words)
24. **A1-24: Countries & Nationalities** (Target: 100 words)
25. **A1-25: Asking for Help** (excuse me, thank you) (Target: 50 words)
26. **A1-26: Classroom Objects** (pen, book, table) (Target: 100 words)
27. **A1-27: 'Have' / 'Have Got'** for possession (Target: 90 words)
28. **A1-28: 'Can' / 'Can't'** for ability (Target: 80 words)
29. **A1-29: Question Words** (What, Where, Who, How) (Target: 90 words)
30. **A1-30: Feelings & Emotions** (Simple) (Target: 80 words)

10. **A2-10: Asking for & Giving Directions** (Target: 90 words)

14. **A2-14: At the Airport** (Check-in, security) (Target: 100 words)
15. **A2-15: At the Hotel** (Checking in/out) (Target: 90 words)
16. **A2-16: Past Holidays & Trips** (Target: 100 words)
17. **A2-17: Adverbs of Frequency** (always, sometimes, never) (Target: 80 words)

25. **A2-25: Modals of Advice** (should) (Target: 80 words)

## **Level B1: Intermediate**

**Goal:** Understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters. Deal with most situations likely to arise whilst travelling. Produce simple connected text on topics which are familiar or of personal interest. Describe experiences, events, dreams, hopes & ambitions.

**Total Target Words:** ~3,000

### B1 Topics
1.  **B1-01: Expressing Opinions, Agreeing & Disagreeing** (Target: 100 words)
2.  **B1-02: Hopes, Dreams & Future Ambitions** (Target: 100 words)
3.  **B1-03: Work & Career** (Responsibilities, promotions) (Target: 120 words)
4.  **B1-04: Education System** (Subjects, exams) (Target: 100 words)
5.  **B1-05: Technology in Daily Life** (Pros & Cons) (Target: 110 words)
6.  **B1-06: Movies & TV Shows** (Genres, reviews) (Target: 100 words)
7.  **B1-07: Books & Literature** (Reading habits) (Target: 100 words)
8.  **B1-08: Music** (Genres, artists, concerts) (Target: 100 words)
9.  **B1-09: Environmental Issues** (Pollution, recycling) (Target: 110 words)
10. **B1-10: Healthy Lifestyles** (Diet, fitness, mental health) (Target: 110 words)
11. **B1-11: Travel & Tourism** (Culture, experiences) (Target: 100 words)
12. **B1-12: Social Media & Digital Communication** (Target: 100 words)
13. **B1-13: Money Management & Budgeting** (Target: 90 words)
14. **B1-14: Present Perfect Tense** ('for' and 'since') (Target: 120 words)
15. **B1-15: Present Perfect vs. Past Simple** (Target: 100 words)
16. **B1-16: Past Perfect Tense** (Narratives) (Target: 100 words)
17. **B1-17: First Conditional** (if + present, will + infinitive) (Target: 100 words)
18. **B1-18: Second Conditional** (if + past, would + infinitive) (Target: 100 words)
19. **B1-19: Reported Speech** (Statements & questions) (Target: 120 words)
20. **B1-20: Passive Voice** (Present & Past Simple) (Target: 100 words)
21. **B1-21: Common Phrasal Verbs** (get up, look for) (Target: 120 words)
22. **B1-22: Giving Advice & Suggestions** (should, ought to, could) (Target: 90 words)
23. **B1-23: Describing Personalities** (Adjectives) (Target: 100 words)
24. **B1-24: Current Events & News** (Vocabulary) (Target: 100 words)
25. **B1-25: Storytelling & Narrating Events** (Connecting words) (Target: 110 words)
26. **B1-26: Politics & Governance** (Basic vocabulary) (Target: 90 words)
27. **B1-27: Making Complaints & Apologizing** (Target: 80 words)
28. **B1-28: Relative Clauses** (who, which, that) (Target: 100 words)
29. **B1-29: Modals of Deduction** (must be, can't be, might be) (Target: 100 words)
30. **B1-30: Comparing Cultures & Traditions** (Target: 100 words)
