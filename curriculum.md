# Gravity English Curriculum: A1 to B1

This document outlines the structured learning path for users, focusing on a clear progression of themes, grammar, and vocabulary.

---

## Level A1: True Beginner

*   **Theme**: Survival English & Basic Introductions
*   **Grammar Focus**:
    *   Simple Present Tense (`be`, `have`)
    *   Subject Pronouns (I, you, he, she, it, we, they)
    *   Basic `Wh-` Questions (What, Where, How)
    *   Singular & Plural Nouns
*   **Vocabulary Concepts**:
    1.  Greetings (Hello, Goodbye)
    2.  Basic Nouns (Man, Woman, City, Country)
    3.  Essential Verbs (is, have, go)
    4.  Numbers (1-10)
    5.  Food & Drink (water, bread, rice)
    6.  Family (mother, father)
    7.  Places (home, school)
    8.  Politeness (please, thank you)
    9.  Colors (red, blue, green)
    10. Days of the Week (Monday, Tuesday)
*   **Unlock Condition**: 0 XP (Available from start)

---

## Level A2: Elementary

*   **Theme**: Daily Routines, Describing the Past, and Making Future Plans
*   **Grammar Focus**:
    *   Present Continuous (`-ing` verbs)
    *   Simple Past Tense (regular and common irregular verbs)
    *   Future with `going to` and `will`
    *   Prepositions of Time and Place (`in`, `on`, `at`)
    *   Object Pronouns (me, you, him, her)
*   **Vocabulary Concepts**:
    1.  Daily Actions (wake up, eat, sleep, work)
    2.  Time (yesterday, today, tomorrow)
    3.  Hobbies & Free Time (music, movie, sport)
    4.  Travel & Transport (bus, train, airport)
    5.  Shopping & Money (buy, sell, price)
    6.  Weather (sunny, rainy, cold)
    7.  Feelings & Emotions (happy, sad, tired)
    8.  Jobs & Occupations (teacher, doctor, student)
    9.  Adjectives of Description (big, small, good, bad)
    10. Conjunctions (and, but, so)
*   **Unlock Condition**: 1,000 XP

---

## Level B1: Intermediate

*   **Theme**: Sharing Opinions, Expressing Ideas, and Narrating Stories
*   **Grammar Focus**:
    *   Present Perfect (`I have been...`)
    *   First & Second Conditionals (`If...`)
    *   Modals of Ability and Advice (`can`, `could`, `should`, `must`)
    *   Comparatives & Superlatives (`bigger than`, `the most interesting`)
    *   Connecting ideas (`because`, `although`, `while`)
*   **Vocabulary Concepts**:
    1.  Opinions & Beliefs (think, believe, agree, disagree)
    2.  Abstract Nouns (love, success, happiness, culture)
    3.  Environment & Nature (planet, pollution, energy)
    4.  Technology & Internet (online, message, download)
    5.  Health & Wellness (exercise, diet, healthy)
    6.  Education & Learning (study, exam, university)
    7.  Goals & Ambitions (dream, plan, achieve)
    8.  Social Issues (community, government, law)
    9.  Personality Adjectives (friendly, honest, creative)
    10. Complex Experiences (traveling, adventure, challenge)
*   **Unlock Condition**: 2,500 XP
